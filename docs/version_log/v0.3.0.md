## 1. Goal

Ship a new standalone tool that helps a Singapore resale condo buyer understand how much upfront cash they need, how much they are short, and how many months of saving it will take to close the gap — with a full resale payment stage simulation and cashflow readiness check at each stage.

---

## 2. Feature — Downpayment Saving Projection (`/downpayment-saving-projection`)

> Plan your resale purchase savings. Adjust property price, CPF, cash, and monthly savings to see how long until you can afford the down payment and whether you can meet each payment stage.

**Inputs** (persisted via `downpaymentSaving` slice):

- **Property**: square feet, PSF, manual value toggle, down payment %, legal fee.
- **Buyer funds** (1–2 buyers): name, current CPF, current cash savings, extra savings / family support, monthly CPF contribution, monthly cash saving.
- **Timeline**: option fee %, exercise fee %, OTP exercise days, stamp duty deadline, completion period in weeks.
- **Scenario toggle**: Conservative (8-week completion, S$8k legal) / Base Case (12 weeks, S$6k legal) / Stretched (16 weeks, S$6k legal).

**Outputs:**

- Summary stat cards: property value, down payment, BSD, legal fee, total payment needed, combined available funds, current deficit/surplus, months needed (2dp).
- Payment timeline table: option fee (Day 0), exercise fee, stamp duty, completion payment with day offsets.
- Cashflow readiness table: available funds at each stage vs cumulative payment due, with Ready/Not Ready status.
- Warnings: property value mismatch (manual vs sqft×PSF), stamp duty liquidity note, deficit amount + months needed, tight timeline alert.

**Engine:** Pure functions in `downpaymentSavingProjection.ts` — no React/Redux dependency.
- `calculatePropertyValue`, `calculateDownpayment`, `buyerSubtotal`, `combinedAvailableFunds`, `combinedMonthlyAccumulation`, `calculateTotalPaymentNeeded`, `calculateCurrentDeficit`, `calculateMonthsNeeded` (2dp), `calculatePaymentStages`, `calculateCashflowReadiness`, `applyScenarioOverrides`.
- `computeDownpaymentSaving` aggregates all outputs from the persisted state.

## 5. Assumptions & Notes

- No ABSD in this tool — purely affordability planning for down payment + BSD + legal fees.
- BSD reuses the existing `calcBSD` from `stampDuty.ts` (Singapore tiered rates).
- Buyer profiles (citizen/PR/foreigner) are not modelled — this tool tracks fund availability only.
- Months needed is `round(deficit / monthlyAccumulation, 2)` — does not round up to the next whole month.
- Scenario toggles only affect `legalFee` and `completionWeeksAfterExercise`; all other timeline fields stay as set.
- Estimates only, not financial advice. Rates configurable in `src/lib/finance/constants.ts`.
