## 2. User Story 1 — Resale Cashflow (`/resale`)

> Buy a resale property starting on a chosen date, project X years forward, toggle
> self-stay vs rent-out, and adjust annual capital growth % and rental growth %.

**Inputs** (persisted via `resale` slice): purchase date, price, down payment %,
interest rate, loan tenure, buyer profile + property count, mode (self-stay / rent-out),
monthly rental, rental growth %, vacancy %, maintenance %, capital appreciation %,
projection horizon.

**Outputs:**
- Summary stats: upfront cash-in, final value, final equity, total net operating cashflow.
- Net position (equity + cashflow − upfront) and a simple return %.
- Composed chart: property value + equity (areas) and cumulative cashflow (line).
- Year-by-year table: value, loan, equity, net cashflow, cumulative.

**Engine:** `projectResale` (existing) + new `summarizeResale` (upfront, final
value/equity, net position, return).

## 3. User Story 2 — New Launch / BUC (`/new-launch`)

> Buy a new project that TOPs in MM/YYYY (today is MM/YYYY). See current down payment
> needed, estimated property price at TOP, expected rental at TOP, and growth potential
> N years after TOP.

**Inputs** (persisted via `newLaunch` slice): today (month), TOP (month), price today,
pre-TOP appreciation %, buyer profile + count, interest rate, loan tenure, estimated
rental at TOP, rental growth %, post-TOP appreciation %, horizon after TOP.

**Outputs:**
- Summary stats: **down payment needed now**, **estimated price at TOP**, **rental yield
  at TOP**, loan amount (75%).
- Cash-needed-now breakdown: booking (5% cash) + S&P (15%) + stamp duty.
- Progressive payment schedule table (stage / % / amount / cumulative).
- Post-TOP projection chart (value, equity, cumulative cashflow).

**Engine:** new `projectNewLaunch` + `monthsBetween` helper.
- `yearsToTop = monthsBetween(today, TOP) / 12`
- `priceAtTop = priceToday × (1 + preTopAppreciation)^yearsToTop`
- `downPaymentNow = booking + S&P + stamp duty`
- `rentalYieldAtTop = annualRent / priceAtTop`
- Post-TOP loan = `priceToday × 75%`, amortized over tenure.

## 6. Assumptions & Notes
- Resale "net position" and "simple return" are estimates; equity already reflects
  principal paydown, so figures are indicative rather than a strict IRR.
- New launch down-payment-now uses booking (5%) + S&P (15%) + stamp duty as the
  immediate cash requirement; later progressive stages are shown in the schedule.
- Pre-TOP appreciation uses fractional years to TOP (months / 12).
- Not financial advice. Rates configurable in `src/lib/finance/constants.ts`.
